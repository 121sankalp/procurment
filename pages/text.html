Manual Procurement of Drugs & Medical Equipment / Devices-2017


Activities of Drugs Procurement

Activities under tendering of generic drugs and other essential medicines, surgical items and other Hospital consumables
S. No. Activity Timeline Responsibility
1 Publication of the Notice Inviting Tender(NIT) and uploading of tender document 0 days Manager(Drugs)
2 Pre bid Meeting from the date of NIT on 7th day Technical specification committee
3 Publication of minutes of Pre-bid meeting and corrigendum if any by 15th day Technical specification committee
4 Opening of technical bids by 30th day Technical evaluation committee
5 Preliminary technical evaluation of the bids by 45th day Technical evaliuation committee though sub committee
6 vetting of Preliminary evaluation report by 48th day Technical evaluation committee
7 Publication of technical evaluation report inviting objections on BMSICL Website by 50th day GM(Procurement)
8 Evaluation of objections by 54th Technical evaluation committee
9 Finalization of technical evaluation report and uploading on BMSICL Website by 55th day Technical evaluation committee
10 Manufacturing site visitand physical verification for technically qualified bidders, Submission of report on site visit and Holding of Technical committee meeting for final inputs and publication of the final evaluation report on BMSICL website by 64th day Technical evaluation committee
11 Notice for opening of Financial bids by 65th day GM(Procurement)
12 Opening of Financial Bids, finalization of L1 bidder and Publication of the Financial Bid report on website of BMSICL by 68th day Final evaluation committee
13 Issue of LOI to L1 Bidder by 75th day GM(Procurement) with approval of MD
